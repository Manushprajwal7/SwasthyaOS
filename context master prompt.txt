SwasthyaOS is India's AI-native healthcare operating system — a unified platform
that serves three distinct user types across the health system:

  1. DOCTORS / CLINICIANS at tertiary hospitals and PHCs
     → Reduces documentation burden by 60%+
     → AI-assisted SOAP notes, ICD-10 coding, discharge summaries
     → Voice-first consultation with real-time transcription
  
  2. ASHA / ANM / FRONTLINE HEALTH WORKERS in rural India
     → Voice-guided triage for low-literacy contexts
     → AI reasoning chain shown step-by-step (explainable AI)
     → Hindi-first, visual-first, low-bandwidth design
     → Generates FHIR referral slips automatically
  
  3. PUBLIC HEALTH OFFICERS / DISTRICT CMOs
     → Real-time syndromic surveillance across 742 districts
     → AI-detected outbreak anomalies with confidence bands
     → Auto-generated situation reports for MOHFW
     → 14-day disease forecast powered by SageMaker

The system is differentiated from generic EMR/HMS software by:
  (a) Deep integration of AWS AI services at every clinical touchpoint
  (b) Explainable AI — every decision shows confidence + reasoning
  (c) India-specific: ABHA ID, ABDM, MOHFW protocols, 12 Indian languages
  (d) Population intelligence layer (not just clinical records)
  (e) Responsible AI audit trail built-in from day one
